## 🎯 Sweet Spot Analysis

**Best engagement:** Users should be able to redeem something meaningful every 2-4 weeks.

**Current system:**
- ✅ Week 1-2: Free Pastry (achievable!)
- ✅ Week 3-4: £5 Voucher (good goal)
- ⚠️ Week 6-8: £10 Voucher (starting to feel long)
- ❌ Month 2-3: Reusable Cup (might lose interest)

**Recommendation:** Current values are good for the lower tiers, but consider reducing high-tier rewards by 30-40% if you want to maintain engagement.

## 📊 Comparison to Old System

**Old System (before beans):**
- Free Pastry: 30 points = 6 days (check-in only)
- £5 Voucher: 50 points = 10 days (check-in only)

**New System (beans):**
- Free Pastry: 1,500 beans = 30 days (check-in only) or 8 days (active)
- £5 Voucher: 4,000 beans = 80 days (check-in only) or 20 days (active)

**Impact:** The new system rewards active users (games + check-ins) much more than passive users (check-in only).

This encourages engagement! 🎮✨
