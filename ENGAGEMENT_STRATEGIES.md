## 1️⃣ Second Chance System (Expired Rewards)

### **The Hook:**
"Your rewards expired... but we're giving you another chance!"

### **How It Works:**
```
User has pending rewards
  ↓
14 days pass, rewards expire
  ↓
Email: "Unfortunately your 15 points expired 😢"
  ↓
"BUT... we're giving you a second chance!"
  ↓
"Visit within 3 days and we'll give you:"
  - 50% of expired points back (7 points)
  - PLUS 5 bonus stamps for coming back
  - PLUS a free game play
  ↓
User visits → Claims second chance reward
  ↓
"Welcome back! Here's your second chance bonus!"
```

### **Psychology:**
- ✅ Loss aversion (they lost something)
- ✅ Redemption opportunity (make it right)
- ✅ Bonus incentive (even better than before)
- ✅ Urgency (only 3 days)

## 💡 Pro Tips

### **1. Rotate Strategies**
Don't do everything at once. Rotate weekly:
- Week 1: Flash sales
- Week 2: Mystery bonus
- Week 3: Streak challenge
- Week 4: Team challenge

### **2. Test & Measure**
Track each strategy:
- Redemption rate
- Visit increase
- Revenue impact
- Customer feedback

### **3. Personalize**
Send different offers to different segments:
- Inactive users: Second chance offers
- Regular users: Streak bonuses
- VIPs: Early access
- New users: Welcome challenges

### **4. Keep It Fresh**
Change offers every 1-2 weeks to maintain excitement
