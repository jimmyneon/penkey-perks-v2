## 1️⃣ Check-In Combo Bonuses

### **The Hook:**
"Check in 3 times this week = Unlock combo bonus!"

### **How It Works:**
```
Monday check-in: +5 points (normal)
Wednesday check-in: +5 points + "2 more for combo!"
Friday check-in: +5 points + COMBO BONUS!
  → Extra 15 points
  → 3 bonus stamps
  → Free game play
  → "You completed the weekly combo!"
```

### **Variations:**
- **Weekend Warrior:** Sat + Sun = bonus
- **Weekday Champion:** Mon-Fri all 5 days = mega bonus
- **Perfect Week:** 7 days straight = legendary reward
