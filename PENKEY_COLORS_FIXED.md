## 🎯 DESIGN PRINCIPLES APPLIED

### **1. Warm Tones Only**
- All colors from amber/orange/yellow family
- No cool colors (blue, purple, green, pink)
- Creates cozy coffee shop atmosphere

### **2. Gradient Variety**
- Different amber/orange combinations
- Visual distinction without rainbow
- Maintains brand consistency

### **3. Text Hierarchy**
- Darkest: amber-950 (headings)
- Dark: amber-900 (subheadings)
- Medium: amber-800 (body)
- Light: amber-700 (accents)

### **4. Icon Colors**
- Match card gradient theme
- Amber-700 or Orange-700
- Consistent with Penkey palette
